Early detection of multiple chronic diseases remains challenging due to fragmented healthcare data, limited interoperability, and lack of transparency in AI-driven diagnostic systems. Most existing medical AI approaches focus on single disease prediction using isolated datasets,
limiting their applicability in real clinical environments involving comorbid conditions. This framework presents PolyDiagAI-Chain, a secure blockchain enabled deep learning framework for unified multi-disease diagnosis and predictive healthcare analytics.The proposed system simultaneously predicts nine heterogeneous diseases using a multimodal learning pipeline that integrates medical imaging, physiological signals, and structured clinical records through unified data fusion. To address the scarcity of
multi-condition datasets, a synthetic patient generation mechanism is introduced to model realistic comorbidity patterns. A temporal disease
progression module further enables future health risk estimation, extending diagnosis toward predictive clinical decision support. Explainable
AI techniques provide interpretable diagnostic insights, while a permissioned blockchain securely records prediction metadata to ensure transparency, integrity, and auditability without affecting inference latency.
Additionally, an AI Trust Score is proposed to quantify diagnostic reliability based on prediction confidence, consistency, and historical performance. Experimental results demonstrate improved robustness and
diagnostic accuracy compared with conventional approaches, highlighting the framework’s potential as a trustworthy and scalable intelligent
healthcare solution.
Hardware Requirements
The following hardware requirements are necessary for efficient execution of the
proposed system:
• Processor: Intel Core i5 or higher
• RAM: Minimum 8 GB (16 GB recommended)
• Storage: Minimum 20 GB free disk space
• GPU (Optional): NVIDIA CUDA-enabled GPU for faster deep learning training
• Network : Stable broadband connection
